Octo AI Bot - Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect
User ID: We collect your Discord user ID to identify and interact with you.
Messages: We collect the messages you send to the bot to process your requests and generate responses.
Server Information: We may collect information about the Discord servers where the bot is used, such as server ID and channel names.
Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the bot, such as the commands you use and the time of your interactions.
2. How We Use Your Information
Provide and Improve Services: We use your information to provide and improve the bot's functionality, including generating responses, processing commands, and personalizing your experience.
Analytics: We may use your information to analyze usage patterns and trends to improve the bot's performance and develop new features.
Communication: We may use your information to communicate with you about the bot, such as sending updates or responding to your inquiries.
3. Data Sharing
Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the bot and providing services to you. These providers are obligated to protect your information and only use it for the purposes we specify.
Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
4. Data Security
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
We use industry-standard encryption technologies to protect your data in transit and at rest.
We restrict access to your information to authorized personnel who need to know that information to provide services to you.
5. User Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
You can request to opt out of data collection or sharing.
You can contact us to exercise your rights or if you have any questions about our privacy practices.
6. Children's Privacy
Octo AI is not intended for children under the age of 16.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
7. Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
Your continued use of the bot after the changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
